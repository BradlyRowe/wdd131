Frequently Asked Questions

What ages are considered "Young Single Adults"?

Our YSA program serves single adults ages 18-30. Everyone in this age range is welcome, whether you're a student, working professional, or in any life stage.

Do I need to be a member to attend?

Not at all! We welcome everyone, regardless of their religious background. Many of our activities are open to friends and community members.

What should I expect at my first visit?

You'll find a friendly, welcoming community. Someone will greet you and help you get oriented. Feel free to participate as much or as little as you're comfortable with.

How can I stay updated on activities?

Follow our announcements, check this website regularly, or reach out to our activities coordinator for the latest updates on events.
