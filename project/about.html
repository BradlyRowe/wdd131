About Our Mission

Building faith, friendship, and community for Young Single Adults

Our Mission

The Roy West YSA program is designed to strengthen young single adults aged 18-30 through spiritual, social, and service opportunities. We believe in building lasting friendships while developing a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ.

Our activities include weekly devotionals, service projects, recreational activities, and social gatherings that help create a supportive community for young adults navigating this important phase of life.

We are committed to creating an inclusive environment where every young adult feels welcomed, valued, and supported in their personal and spiritual growth journey.

Spiritual Growth

Weekly devotionals, scripture study, and gospel discussions to strengthen faith and testimony.

Service Opportunities

Community service and humanitarian projects that make a difference in our local area.

Social Activities

Fun events, recreational opportunities, and social gatherings to build lasting friendships.

What We Do

Weekly Devotionals

Every Sunday evening, we gather for inspiring devotionals featuring guest speakers, testimonies, and spiritual discussions.

  • Gospel-centered messages
  • Personal testimonies
  • Interactive discussions
  • Musical performances

Service Projects

Monthly opportunities to serve our community and those in need, following Christ's example of love and compassion.

  • Community clean-up projects
  • Food bank volunteering
  • Elderly assistance programs
  • Humanitarian aid projects

Social Activities

Fun recreational events designed to build friendships and create positive memories together.

  • Outdoor adventures
  • Game nights
  • Cultural events
  • Seasonal celebrations

Educational Programs

Workshops and classes focused on personal development, life skills, and spiritual growth.

  • Financial literacy workshops
  • Relationship building classes
  • Career development seminars
  • Gospel study groups

Ready to Get Involved?
