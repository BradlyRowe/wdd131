Roy West Stake – Young Single Adults (YSA) Site Plan

Site Name

Roy West Stake - YSA – A central gathering place for Young Single Adults in the Roy West Stake. This name reflects both the geographic and spiritual focus of the site, emphasizing unity, purpose, and belonging.

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a central resource for Young Single Adults in the Roy West Stake. It will provide:

  • A welcoming homepage with spiritual and social messaging
  • An “About Us” section outlining the mission and vision of the YSA program
  • A “Gathering Place” page with upcoming events, activities, and service opportunities
  • A “Contact” page for outreach, support, and leadership connections
  • Admin-controlled content updates via JSON configuration
  • Responsive and accessible design hosted on GitHub Pages

Scenarios

  • “Where can I find upcoming YSA activities in the Roy West Stake?”
  • “How do I get in touch with YSA leaders or volunteer for an event?”
  • “What is the purpose of the YSA program and how can I get involved?”

Color Schema

Primary Palette

#003366 - Dark Navy Blue (Primary brand & navigation)
#0055aa - Medium Blue (Hover states & accents)
#f5f5f5 - Light Gray (Background)

Secondary Palette

#3498db - Sky Blue (Links & highlights)
#e74c3c - Coral Red (Alerts & CTAs)
#27ae60 - Forest Green (Success states)

Neutral Palette

#ffffff - Pure White (Cards & content)
#ecf0f1 - Cloud Gray (Subtle backgrounds)
#95a5a6 - Silver Gray (Borders & dividers)
#34495e - Charcoal (Footer & low contrast text)

Color Usage Guidelines

  • Dark Navy Blue (#003366): Primary brand color for headers, footers, navigation, and main headings
  • Medium Blue (#0055aa): For hover states, interactive highlights, and navigation accents
  • Sky Blue (#3498db): For links, secondary highlights, and informational elements
  • Coral Red (#e74c3c): For important alerts, error states, and call-to-action buttons
  • Forest Green (#27ae60): For success messages, confirmations, and positive feedback
  • Neutral colors: For backgrounds, content cards, borders, and supporting elements

Typography

  • Headings: 'Montserrat', sans-serif – clean and modern
  • Body: 'Segoe UI', sans-serif – readable and familiar
  • Special Sections: 'Playfair Display', serif – used for quotes or spiritual emphasis

Responsive Design Notes

  • Breakpoints: Mobile (320px-768px), Tablet (768px-1024px), Desktop (1024px+)
  • Navigation: Hamburger menu collapses on mobile; full menu bar on desktop
  • Layout: Single column stacking on mobile; multi-column grid on desktop
  • Images: Responsive sizing with appropriate aspect ratios for each viewport
  • Typography: Scalable font sizes and line heights for optimal readability
  • Touch Targets: Minimum 44px touch targets on mobile devices

Testing

Site will be validated using:

  • HTML and CSS validators
  • Accessibility tools (e.g., Lighthouse, WAVE)
  • Performance and SEO audits
  • Color contrast checkers
